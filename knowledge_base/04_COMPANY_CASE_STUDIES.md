## CLASSIFICATION BY MANIPULATION TECHNIQUE

### Revenue/Profit Inflation
- **Indo Count**: Asset revaluation inflating Rs 178.7 cr PBT
- **Escorts**: Rs 1,000+ cr bypassed P&L via BRR
- **HEG**: Rs 192 cr forex losses hidden in fixed assets
- **Globus Spirits**: Rs 36 cr advertising capitalized as intangible

### Cash Flow Manipulation
- **Sreeleathers**: Rs 11.5 cr loan shown as CFO
- **PIX Transmissions**: Rs 134 cr asset sale shown under CFO
- **Supreme Industries**: Interest under CFO instead of CFF
- **HEG**: Rs 55.5 cr CFO calculation error in annual report

### Promoter Self-Dealing
- **Rexnord**: Warrants at 43% discount, control gained cheaply
- **Sreeleathers**: Circular shareholding via Shoeline Trading
- **Atul**: Sold Amal stake to promoters at point of turnaround
- **Stovec**: Sold sugar screen assets to group, leased them back
- **Kokuyo Camlin**: Pre-school business sold to promoters at 1/10th

### Structural Fraud Indicators
- **Omkar Speciality**: Declining raw material consumption with rising product sales
- **Minda Industries**: Selective consolidation to hide losses
- **Escorts**: Company-owned shares shown as promoter holding
