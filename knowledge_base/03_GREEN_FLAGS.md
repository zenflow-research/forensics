# Green Flags: Positive Indicators
## Signs of a Well-Run, Investment-Worthy Company

---

## FINANCIAL GREEN FLAGS

### Cash Flow Excellence
| Indicator | Benchmark | Examples |
|-----------|-----------|---------|
| cCFO > cPAT over 10 years | Ratio > 1.0 | TVS Srichakra (cCFO 2x cPAT), Finolex Industries |
| Positive FCF consistently | FCF > 0 for 8+ of 10 years | MRF (Rs 1,897 cr), Finolex Cables (Rs 1,461 cr) |
| Dividends funded from FCF | Dividends < FCF | Divi's Labs, Supreme Industries |
| Debt reduction over decade | Lower absolute debt | Multiple companies becoming debt-free |

### Profitability Strength
| Indicator | Benchmark | Examples |
|-----------|-----------|---------|
| Stable/improving OPM | Variation < 5 pp over 10 years | Supreme Industries (14-16%), Divi's Labs (37-40%) |
| Pricing power demonstrated | Raw material % of sales stable | Bharat Rasayan (65-66%), TVS Srichakra |
| SSGR > Actual Growth Rate | Comfortable margin | Finolex Cables (SSGR 25-35% vs growth 5-7%) |
| Value creation > retained earnings | Market cap increase / retained earnings > 1 | MRF (Rs 3.89 per Rs 1), NOCIL (Rs 7.5 per Rs 1) |

### Balance Sheet Strength
| Indicator | Benchmark | Examples |
|-----------|-----------|---------|
| Debt-free or declining debt | D/E approaching zero | AIA Engineering, Stovec Industries |
| Credit rating upgrades | Consistent upgrades over years | TVS Srichakra, NOCIL (A to AA), Indo Count (6 notches) |
| Growing cash reserves from operations | Not from debt | AIA Engineering (Rs 303 cr to Rs 1,573 cr) |
| Working capital improving | Receivables days declining, ITR improving | Caplin Point (69 to 18 days receivables) |

## OPERATING EFFICIENCY GREEN FLAGS

| Indicator | What It Means |
|-----------|--------------|
| NFAT in 2-5 range, stable or improving | Efficient asset utilization without commodity-level competition |
| Near-zero receivables days | Cash & carry model (strong bargaining power) |
| ITR improving over time | Better inventory management |
| Capacity utilization > 80% | Efficient use of existing assets before expanding |
| Formula-based pricing with customers | Automatic raw material cost pass-through |

## THE IDEAL COMPANY PROFILE

A company scoring well across all green flags would have:
1. **SSGR >> Actual Growth** (can self-fund easily)
2. **Consistent positive FCF** (no need for external capital)
3. **cCFO >= cPAT** (profits convert to cash)
4. **Stable OPM > 15%** (pricing power)
5. **NFAT 2-5 range** (efficient but not commodity)
6. **Declining or zero debt** with growing cash
7. **Promoter salary < 4% of PAT** with increasing dividends
8. **Succession plan** in place
9. **No questionable related party transactions**
10. **P/E < 10-11** for margin of safety
