## CATEGORY A: FINANCIAL STATEMENT RED FLAGS

### A1. Revenue & Profit Manipulation
| # | Red Flag | How to Detect | Real Example |
|---|----------|---------------|-------------|
| 1 | Revenue recognized at factory dispatch, not buyer acceptance | Read revenue recognition policy in notes | Indo Count Industries |
| 2 | Percentage of Completion Method (POCM) inflating revenue | Check unbilled revenue, "Due to customers" balance | KNR Constructions, MBL Infrastructure |
| 3 | Capitalizing revenue expenses as intangible assets | Check intangible asset additions for advertising/brand costs | Globus Spirits (Rs 36 cr advertising capitalized) |
| 4 | Asset revaluation to inflate balance sheet | Check revaluation reserves, compare to depreciation | Indo Count (Rs 178.7 cr inflated PBT), NOCIL |
| 5 | Transferring general reserve to P&L to offset amortization | Check reserve movements in equity schedule | Emami Limited |
| 6 | Bypass P&L via Business Reconstruction Reserve | Check if write-offs adjusted directly to reserves | Escorts (Rs 1,000+ cr bypassed P&L) |
| 7 | Non-operating income forming large portion of profits | Break down "Other Income" line items | Multiple companies |
| 8 | Classifying recurring losses as "Exceptional Items" | Track exceptional items over multiple years | HEG (forex losses) |

## QUICK-SCAN RED FLAG SCORING

### Priority 1 (Immediate Disqualifiers)
- Negative SSGR with aggressive growth
- cCFO << cPAT over 10 years
- Dividends from negative FCF
- Credit rating downgrade / rating shopping
- Promoter enrichment via warrants at deep discount

### Priority 2 (Serious Concerns)
- Promoter salary > 10% of PAT
- Related party loans at non-market rates
- Multiple cash flow misclassifications
- Declining NFAT with increasing debt
- Failed diversification history

### Priority 3 (Monitor Closely)
- Single customer/supplier dependency
- Tax holidays expiring
- Fluctuating OPM (>10 pp range)
- Annual report errors/inconsistencies
- Rising receivables days
